## Project Overview
The project focuses on analyzing business performance using interactive Tableau dashboards and data-driven analytics. Using a dataset containing over 51,000 business transactions, the project explores sales performance, profitability, customer segmentation, product categories, and geographic trends to support strategic business decision-making.

The objective of the projectc is to transform raw business data into actionable insight through visualization, forecasting and statistical analysis

## Business Questions
This project aims to answer the following questions:
1. Which products and categories generate the highest sales and profits?
2. Which customer segments contribute the most to business performance?
3. How do discounts affects profitability?
4. What sales trends can be observed over time?
5. How can future sales performance be forecasted?

## Project Workflow
1. Data Preparation
   cleaned and validated transactional data
   reviewed missing values and inconsistencies
   prepared datasets for visualization and analysis

2. Business Performance Analysis
   analyzed sales and profit performance across products and categories
   evaluated customer segment contributions to revenue and profitability
   identified geographic regions with strong and weak performance

3. Profitability Analysis
   investigated relationships between discounting strategies and profit margins
   performed regression analysis to quantidy the impact of discounts on profitability

4. Dashboard Development
   created interactive Tableau dashboards to visualize

5. Forecasting Analysis

## Dashboard Features
Executive Overview
Provides a high-level summary of sales, profit, and business performance.

Sales Analysis
Tracks revenue trends over time and highlights top-performing products and regions.

Profitability Dashboard
Identifies key drivers of profit and evaluates the impact of discounts.

Customer Segmentation
Analyzes purchasing behavior across customer groups.

Forecasting Dashboard
Projects future sales performance using historical trends.

## Key Findings
1. Certain product categories generated high revenue but lower profitability due to discounting practices.
2. Customer segments displayed significant differences in purchasing behavior and profit contribution.
3. Geographic analysis revealed regional variations in sales and profitability.
4. Discounting showed a strong impact on profit margins, supporting the need for optimized pricing strategies.
5. Forecasting analysis provided valuable insights for future business planning.
